Matrices

Matrices are structures that organize numbers into a rectangular array, allowing operations to be performed on groups of values simultaneously. We can represent linear transformatinos using matrices. They are a foundational concept in linear algebra and are wide used in computer graphics where they are used for transformations, projections, and modeling spatial relationships.
