## Gcodes

The gcodes are the language used to tell the print what to do. You can save a sequence of gcodes in the memory card or having the Octoprint sending them on the fly while the printer is printing. You can also send single gcodes commands from the terminal of Octoprint.

## Slicer

The slicer software is responsible for slicing your 3d model (.stl) to generate gcodes for your printer.
